What starts small can transform the world. But it's important to remember that this isn't an individualist, rags-to-riches, or "pull yourself up by your bootstraps" narrative. Inherent in movements emerging from the bottom is the idea of collective action. One person is not autocratically making decisions from an arbitrary position of power.
